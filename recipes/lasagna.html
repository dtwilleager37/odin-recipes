Lasagna Recipe

This is one of my favorite meals, lasagna! This picture and receipe comes from: https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/23600/worlds-best-lasagna/

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps to Make Lasagna

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recripe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
