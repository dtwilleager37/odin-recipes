Grilled Cheese Recipe

This is one of my favorite meals, grilled cheese! This picture and receipe comes from: https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/23891/grilled-cheese-sandwich/

Ingredients

  • 4 Slices white bread
  • 3 Tablespoons butter
  • 2 Slices Cheddar Cheese

Steps to Make Grilled Cheese Sandwich

  1. Preheat nonstick skillet over medium heat
  2. Generously butter one side of a slice of bread
  3. Place butter side down in skillet
  4. Add 1 slice of cheese
  5. Butter second slice of bread on one side
  6. Place butter side up on top of cheese
  7. Cook until lightly browned on one side
  8. Flip over and cook other side until cheese is melted
  9. Repeat with other 2 slices of bread
