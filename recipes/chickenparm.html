Chicken Parmesan Recipe

This is one of my favorite meals, chicken parmesan! This picture and receipe comes from: https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/223042/chicken-parmesan/

Ingredients

  • 4 Skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • Salt and freshley ground pepper to taste
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup prepared tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup fresh mozzarella
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/2 cup grated provolone cheese
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil

Steps to Make Lasagna

  1. Preheat an oven to 450 degrees F
  2. Cover chicken with plastic wrap and pound flat
  3. Season chicken and coat with flour
  4. Beat eggs in shallow bowl and set aside
  5. Mix bread crumbs and cheese
  6. Dip chicken in eggs, then to bread crumbs, and then place on rack for 10 minutes
  7. Heat oil in pan on stove
  8. Cook chicken in oil for two minutes on each side
  9. Place in baking dish and put on remaining cheese
  10. Cook until internal temperature in 165 degrees F
  11. Remove and cool for 10 minutes
